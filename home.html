Saudi Marketplace

Empowering Sauti Africa business owners and providing business oppurtunities.

What we do

We provide an online platform for Sauti African business owners and merchants to buy and sell produce. We try and place an emphasis on assisting female merchants seeing that the majority of merchants are women. We aim to improve their business and increase their economic oppurtunities to grow out of poverty.

Our Services

With our user friendly, mobile based platform, any merchant or business owner with a mobile device can use and access our platform. They will have access to exchange rates

Merchants and business owners can utilize our platform by creating an account and begin listing produce for sale.
